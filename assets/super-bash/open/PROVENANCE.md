# Original Super Bash Folds assets

The files in this directory were created specifically for Super Bash Folds.
They are not conversions, captures, or adaptations of assets from another
game. The 20 item icons and two interface cursors were created with
**GPT-5.6-Sol**, under the direction and review of project maintainer Swarek.

Contributors dedicate these files to the public domain under
[CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/). Future
contributions to this directory must be original, include an updated
`SHA256SUMS`, and follow the policy in
[`ASSET_POLICY.md`](https://github.com/Swarek/Super_Bash_Folds/blob/main/ASSET_POLICY.md).

This declaration does not apply to fighters, stages, sounds, or third-party
dependencies, which have their own provenance files.
