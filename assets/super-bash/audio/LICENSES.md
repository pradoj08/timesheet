# Open audio sources

Only files under `open/` are part of the distributable game. The local
development overlay may contain other ignored audio folders; those folders are
not licensed or shipped by this repository.

## Kenney sound effects and jingles

- Creator: Kenney
- License: [CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)
- Source packs:
  - [Impact Sounds](https://kenney.nl/assets/impact-sounds)
  - [Interface Sounds](https://kenney.nl/assets/interface-sounds)
  - [Music Jingles](https://kenney.nl/assets/music-jingles)
- Selected material: menu cues, impacts, movement cues, countdown cues and
  short match-state jingles under `open/sfx/`
- Modifications: selected files were renamed; the OGG payloads were not
  recompressed or otherwise modified

The license text distributed with each upstream pack is preserved under
`open/licenses/`.

## Two Simple Game Music Loops

- Creator: qubodup
- License: [CC0 1.0](https://creativecommons.org/publicdomain/zero/1.0/)
- Source: [OpenGameArt](https://opengameart.org/content/two-simple-game-music-loops)
- Files:
  - `menumusicloop-tiggo.ogg` → `open/music/menu-loop.ogg`
  - `levelmusicloop-tigrun.ogg` → `open/music/battle-loop.ogg`
- Modifications: renamed only; no recompression

Exact runtime-file hashes are recorded in `open/SHA256SUMS`.
